How to Split Utilities With Roommates Fairly

Rent is only half the story. Utilities can add hundreds per month — and splitting them wrong causes just as much conflict as unfair rent.

The Three Main Methods

Method 1: Equal Split

Everyone pays the same share of every bill. Simple and easy to track. Works best when roommates have similar habits and usage patterns.

Method 2: Usage-Based Split

Each roommate pays based on their actual usage. Fairest in theory, but nearly impossible to measure accurately for most utilities.

Method 3: One Person Per Bill

Each roommate owns one utility and pays it directly, then others reimburse them. Reduces transactions and gives each person responsibility.

Which Split Works for Each Utility

  • Electricity — Equal split unless usage is dramatically different
  • Internet — Equal split always; it is a fixed cost regardless of who uses it more
  • Water — Equal split; nearly impossible to track individual usage
  • Streaming services — Whoever uses it pays; do not force roommates to split subscriptions they do not want
