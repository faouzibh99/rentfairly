Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026. RentFairly is committed to your privacy. This page explains what data we collect and how we use it.

What Data We Collect

RentFairly does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the calculator. The rent and room details you enter are processed entirely in your browser and are not sent to our servers.

Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymous usage data such as pages visited and time spent. No personally identifiable information is collected. You can opt out of Google Analytics using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google AdSense

We display ads via Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can manage your ad preferences at adssettings.google.com.

Shared Links

When you use the "Share Result" feature, your rent split data is encoded directly into the URL. This data is not stored on our servers — it exists only in the URL you share.

Third-Party Links

Our blog may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: privacy@rentfairly.app
