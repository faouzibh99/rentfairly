25 Questions to Ask Before Moving In With a Roommate

The questions you do not ask before moving in become the arguments you have six months later. Here are the 25 you cannot skip.

Advertisement
Ad Space — 728x90

About Money

  1. How do you propose we split rent — equally or based on room size?
  2. What day of the month will you pay your share?
  3. How do you prefer to pay — bank transfer, cash, or app?
  4. Have you ever been unable to pay rent on time? What happened?
  5. How should we handle shared expenses like cleaning supplies?

About Daily Life

  1. What are your working hours — are you home during the day?
  2. Do you cook often? Are you okay sharing kitchen space?
  3. How often do you clean, and how clean do you need the apartment to be?
  4. Do you smoke, indoors or outdoors?
  5. Do you have any pets or are you planning to get any?
  6. What time do you usually go to sleep?
  7. How loud do you listen to music or watch TV?

About Guests

  1. How often do you have friends over?
  2. Do you have a partner who might stay over regularly?
  3. Are you okay with the other person having guests without advance notice?
  4. How do you feel about parties or gatherings at the apartment?

About Living Preferences

  1. How do you feel about shared food — separate or communal?
  2. Are you sensitive to smells, cooking, or strong cleaning products?
  3. Do you work from home and need quiet during certain hours?
  4. How do you prefer to handle disagreements — directly or in writing?

About the Future

  1. How long are you planning to stay?
  2. What would you do if you needed to leave before the lease ends?
  3. Would you be open to a written roommate agreement?
  4. Is there anything about your lifestyle I should know before we move in?
  5. What was your last living situation like, and why did it end?
