About RentFairly

RentFairly was built to solve the most common — and most avoidable — roommate conflict: unfair rent splits.

Why We Built This

It started with a viral Reddit post. Someone described living in the smallest room in their apartment, with one tiny window facing a dumpster, no closet, and constant street noise — paying the exact same rent as their roommate who had a large room, a private bathroom, and a walk-in closet.

The post got over 10,000 upvotes because almost everyone recognised the situation. Equal rent splits are deeply common and deeply unfair. But no one had built a simple, free, shareable tool to fix this.

So we built one.

How It Works

RentFairly uses a weighted scoring algorithm that evaluates each room based on square footage, natural light, noise level, bathroom access, closet space, and extra perks. Each room's score as a proportion of the total score determines its fair share of rent.

The calculation happens entirely in your browser. We do not store your data. The shareable link encodes your results directly in the URL — no account, no database, no tracking.

Our Mission

To make fair rent splits the default, not the exception. Every roommate deserves to pay for what they actually get — no more, no less.

Free, Always

RentFairly is and will always be free to use. We are supported by non-intrusive advertising that keeps the lights on without charging users anything.
